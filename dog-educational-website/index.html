Featured Breeds

Golden Retriver

Friendly, intelligent and devoted.

  • Temperature: Friendly, intelligent, devoted, outgoing, eager to please. Excellent family dogs.
  • Size: Medium-large.
  • Energy Level: High; require regular exercise and mental stimulation.
  • Training: Highly trainable due to their intelligence and desire to please. Excel in obedience, agility, and as service dogs.
  • Care: Moderate grooming needs (regular brushing). Prone to certain health issues (hips, elbows, cancer).
  • Common Uses: Family companion, therapy dog, guide dog, hunting/retrieving, search and rescue.

German Shepherd

Key attributes of breed

  • Temperature: Loyal, courageous, confident, intelligent, and protective. Can be aloof with strangers but deeply devoted to their family.
  • Size: Large.
  • Energy Level: High; need significant daily exercise and mental challenges to prevent boredom.
  • Training: Highly intelligent and trainable; thrive with consistent training and early socialization.
  • Care: Moderate grooming (shed a lot). Generally hardy, but prone to hip and elbow dysplasia.
  • Common Uses: Police and military work, search and rescue, service dogs, herding, family protector, loyal companion.
Learn More

Belgian Malinois

Key attributes of breed

  • Temperature: Intense, highly intelligent, confident, hardworking, and energetic. Can be reserved with strangers. Not for novice owners.
  • Size: Medium-large.
  • Energy Level: Extremely high; require immense amounts of physical and mental exercise daily.
  • Training: Exceptional work ethic and trainability; excel in advanced obedience, protection sports, and working roles. Early, extensive socialization is crucial.
  • Care: Low grooming needs. Generally healthy.
  • Common Uses: Police and military K9, protection work, search and rescue, competitive dog sports (schutzhund, agility).

Siberian Husky

Key attributes of breed

  • Temperature: Independent, mischievous, friendly, adventurous, and intelligent. Known for being escape artists.
  • Size: Medium.
  • Energy Level: High; bred for endurance, require a lot of exercise.
  • Training: Intelligent but notoriously independent and strong-willed; can be challenging to train, especially recall.
  • Care: High grooming needs (heavy seasonal shedding). Adaptable to cold climates.
  • Common Uses: Sledding, companionship, can excel in various dog sports with dedicated training.

Bullmastiff

Key attributes of breed

  • Temperature: Loyal, protective, courageous, calm, and affectionate with family. Can be reserved with strangers.
  • Size: Large to giant.
  • Energy Level: Moderate; need daily walks but not extreme exercise. Enjoy lounging once exercised.
  • Training: Intelligent but can be stubborn; require firm, consistent training and early socialization.
  • Care: Low grooming needs. Prone to certain health issues (hip/elbow dysplasia, bloat, cancer).
  • Common Uses: Family guardian, companion.
Learn More

Cane Corso

Key attributes of breed

  • Temperature: Assertive, confident, intelligent, protective, and affectionate with their family. Can be dominant and require experienced handling.
  • Size: Large to giant.
  • Energy Level: Moderate to high; need regular exercise and mental stimulation.
  • Training: Highly intelligent and trainable but require a confident, consistent leader and extensive early socialization. Not for first-time dog owners.
  • Care: Low grooming needs. Prone to hip/elbow dysplasia, bloat.
  • Common Uses: Guardian, protection dog, working dog, companion for experienced owners.
Learn More

Akita

Key attributes of breed

  • Temperature: Loyal, protective, dignified, courageous, and reserved with strangers. Can be dominant with other dogs.
  • Size: Large.
  • Energy Level: Moderate; enjoy daily walks and playtime but don't require constant high-impact exercise.
  • Training: Intelligent but very independent and strong-willed. Require early, consistent socialization and a firm, respectful leader.
  • Care: Moderate grooming (heavy seasonal shedding). Generally healthy, but prone to certain autoimmune conditions.
  • Common Uses: Guardian, companion. Known for loyalty and bravery.
